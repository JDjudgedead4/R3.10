Our values

Britteny quality

We are committed to preparing our crepes with ingredients local and authentic of the highest quality, perpetuating the traditional Breton recipes. Each pancake is carefully crafted to offer you an authentic taste experience, directly inspired by Breton tradition.

Artisanal Tradition

We celebrate the artisanal tradition of making crepes, by preserving ancestral artisanal cooking methods on bilig (Breton pancake plate). Each crepe is prepared with attention to detail and love to honor this unique culinary heritage of Brittany.

respecting the environment

We are determined to minimize our impact on the environment. We favor local suppliers to support the economy regionally and adopt practices that respect the environment, in particular by reducing waste and using sustainable packaging.

Warm Service

Our team is dedicated to providing warm and welcoming service. personalized, typical of Breton hospitality. We welcome each customer with a smile and we do everything we can to make your experience with us most pleasant.

Community Engagement

We are proud of our link with the Breton community. In addition to serve delicious crepes, we are actively involved in local events and support cultural initiatives and social sectors of our region to contribute to its dynamism and its flourishing.
